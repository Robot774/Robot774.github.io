I am Boyu Chen (陈博俣), an undergraduate student at the [Department of Automation](https://www.au.tsinghua.edu.cn/), [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/).

I will be joining [MMLab@HKU](https://www.mmlab-ntu.com/) at the [University of Hong Kong](https://www.hku.hk/) as a PhD student in Fall 2026, fortunately advised by Prof. [Ping Luo](http://luoping.me/), Prof. [Hongyang Li](https://lihongyang.info/), and Prof. [Yi Ma](https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~yima/).

Research Interests
======
My research interests lie at the intersection of **Robotics**, **Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models**, and **World Models**. I am particularly interested in building generalist agents that can perceive, reason, and act in the physical world.
